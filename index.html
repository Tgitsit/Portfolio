Featured projects

Below are curated examples of my recent work. My goal with the choices below is to showcase my growth as a creative director and product designer.

Idea Board

Idea Board is a note-taking application that gives you an infinite canvas, so you can visualize the big picture. The challenge here was to narrow down the focus and find a niche market. Through this project, I learned that the right product is not about giving every possible choice to the users — but about making the right choices, so they have a clutter-free experience.

Burgen

A motion graphics project uses the same design principles as any UX project. Content and design overlap in so many ways! Amongst other things, this project taught me that it is OK to pause and recalibrate to get the best results. The final video won us a prestigious award.
