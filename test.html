User Persona

The user survey clarified a number of questions I had in regards to the target market. Based on the results of the survey, I came up with two personas that we’ll be designing this product for.

Marc Gross

41, Chicago, IL

Digital Marketing Director at a fortune 500 company

Marc Gross

41, Chicago, IL

Digital Marketing Director at a fortune 500 company

Goals

  • To save and manage a list of articles, images and videos from the Internet.

Biography

Marc lives in a suburban neighborhood in Chicago, IL. He’s a father of three, and a digital marketing director at a mid-sized company. He uses Google Drive and dropbox paper, but wishes that there was a less rigid solution to help visually strategize with his team.

Frustrations

  • Current app doesn’t provide a good way to store articles, other than a bunch of links.

  • Current app doesn’t provide a good way to track changes or leave comments within images or videos.

“Saving inspiration links without being to preview them is a waste of time"

Anna Harris

24, Los Angeles, CA

Freelance Graphic Designer

Goals

  • To find a better way to create and present moodboards and other visual elements.

Biography

Anna lives in Los Angeles, CA and works as a freelance Graphic Designer and Animator for various clients ranging from small businesses to big corporations. Anna typically uses Dropbox and One drive for media backup and a service like Canva to create moodboards and presentations. The ability to create visual presentations as well as save her files in one location will make her process quite effiient.

Frustrations

  • Current app doesn’t provide a good way to visually save media (images/videos) found online.

  • Current app’s document creation is quite basic and rigid.

“Saving inspiration links without being to preview them is a waste of time"
